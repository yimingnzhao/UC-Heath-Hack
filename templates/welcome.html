Readmission Avoidance

What Is Readmission?

A readmission is when a patient needs to return to the hospital less then 30 days after being discharged. Many factors may lead to hospital readmissions, such as, poor quality care or a gap in the transition between different providers and care settings. Readmissions may also occur if patients are discharged from hospitals prematurely, if they are discharged to inappropriate settings, or if they do not receive adequate information or resources to aid in recovery. Patients can help avoid readmission by making sure they understand their care plan before they are discharged from the hospital. They also need to be sure to follow up on care once they leave the hospital.

SO YOU WANNA BE HEALTHY

Of course, you do. Living healthy reduces your risks of injury, hospitalization, complications, and death. But it seems physically intimidating and incredibly expensive. Social media influencers and celebrities pitch Miami Beach diet plans, $15 superfood smoothies, four-hour hardcore workouts, and yoga on the beach to connect with your inner self. However, you do not need to do all of these things to be healthy! If you follow some key rules, you can be living a healthy life yourself! Be sure to exercise. Exercise keeps you in shape both physically and mentally. Whether it be going for a nice stroll around the block or pumping iron right after a kickboxing session, do what feels right for you but be sure to put as much effort into your exercises as possible and follow a workout schedule. If you physically can, try to add more to your workouts as you become more physically fit. You should also start eating healthier. While a few slices of pizza or a burger won’t kill you if you eat them once in a while, try to avoid junk food for the most part. Have a diverse diet of fruits, vegetables, grains, and more. Look at USDA’s MyPlate for a better idea of the split you should be aiming. Exercise daily if you can, and have a nutritious and diverse diet. Follow these, and you will be closer to living a truly healthy lifestyle!
